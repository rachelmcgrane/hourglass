01.

A Touch of Magic

It’s simple: turn over the cordless hourglass to begin a reading session. Move it to wherever they read and return it to its charging dock anytime.

02.

Becoming Brighter

The soft LED light trickles from top to bottom and changes colors the more your child reads. It can also serve as a night light.

03.

All In Sync

The Hourglass connects via bluetooth to your mobile phone. Add your child’s reading session data to the Beanstack mobile app to track reading and earn virtual badges.
